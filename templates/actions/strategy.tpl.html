  • 1
    Foundation

    The foundation and utilities will be identified and marked. The site will be located and excavated. Foundation walls are poured.

  • 2
    Framing

    Walls, floors and roof framing are completed. Window and door openings are positioned according to blueprints.

  • 3
    Roofing

    Shingles, windows and exterior sheathing are installed. Aluminum or vinyl gutters are installed as well.

  • 4
    Dry Wall

    Electrical, Heating, Ventilation (HVAC) and plumbing systems are in. Siding application and cornice work will have been completed.

  • 5
    Wood Trim & Doors

    Interior doors are set and the moldings are installed. Staircase railings are added.

  • 6
    Painting

    A primer coat and finish coat is applied to all walls. Proper paint or other wall covering is applied according to the specifications.

  • 7
    Cabinetry & Countertops

    Kitchen cabinets are set and its countertops installed.

  • 8
    Finalize Your Design

    Final plumbing and electrical fixtures will be set. The flooring and/or carpeting will be installed.

  • 9
    Punch-out

    Completion check for interior and exterior finishing of the house.
